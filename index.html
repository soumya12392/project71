campus life

school library

this is the library

Our school has a very large library. There are plenty number of books in the library. We have a librarian who keeps a track of all the books that come in and go out of the library. We have an exclusive library period, where we are allowed to go and read books.

From small story books to large reference books, journals and magazines are all available in our library. They are all arranged in different sections, so that it becomes easy for those who search for a particular book. It helps students refer notes that they are looking for and helps in cultivating a reading habit too.

school playground

this is the playground

In my school, there is a big playground. The ground is covered with huge boundary walls. My school playground is divided into two sections. One section is for playing football and other sports and another is for playing basketball.

My school playground also has many trees under which students sit and study sometimes or take rest. Also at the corner of the playground, there are three sports rooms which are equipped with the latest sports items. My school playground is very green and beautiful.
